Credit Card Check

Description

This check performs the following actions to validate credit card numbers:
LUHN Formula (Mod 10) for Validation of Primary Account Number
The following steps are required to validate the primary account number:
Step 1: Double every second digit from right to left
Step 2: Add each individual/single digit
Step 3: If the sum modulo 10 is equal to 0 (total ends in zero), then the number is valid.

For example, to validate the primary account number 371449635398431:
Step 1:

CC # 3 7 1 4 4 9 6 3 5 3 9 8 4 3 1
Double every 2nd number (RTL) x2 x2 x2 x2 x2 x2 x2
Result 3 14 1 8 4 18 6 6 5 6 9 16 4 6 1
New number: 314184186656916461
Step 2: 3 + (1+4) + 1 + (8) + 4 + (1+8) + 6 + (6) + 5 + (6) + 9 + (1+6) + 4 + (6) + 1
Step 3: Sum = 80; 80 mod 10 = 0 (80 ends in 0); Card number is valid.

Data Input


Note: A green shadow means the credit card number passed the check. Red = failure.
